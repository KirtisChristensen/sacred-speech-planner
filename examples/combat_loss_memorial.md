# Example: Combat Loss Memorial

A field memorial for a soldier lost in operations, attended by the unit and family.

## Scenario description (paste into Step 1)

> Field memorial in 36 hours for a soldier killed in the line of duty during a routine mission. Roughly 80 soldiers from the same company will attend, plus a handful of family members who flew in. The unit is grieving and angry; some of them were on the mission and are processing it themselves. Family is bereaved and looking for meaning. I have 10 to 15 minutes. Mixed faith backgrounds across the unit. The chaplain tradition I'm working from is Christian, but I want this to land for everyone present. I plan to anchor in Psalm 23 but keep the language accessible. Need to honor the soldier without re-traumatizing the team that was there.
